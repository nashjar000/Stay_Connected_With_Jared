The Mission - Highlights

I decided to serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I learned a lot, grew a lot, and grew my testimony that Jesus Christ lives and that he truly loves each of us. I have a video journal from the mission--mostly just the goofy parts--don't worry, I was a good missionary! (The COVID pandemic hit just a little after I got to the mission field, so, that was a good experience, too.)
